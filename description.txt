Another QoL mod... but this one is a little different.

This mod aims to condense most of the other 1-off QoL mods into one.

All features are configurable.

Current Features:
 - Endless Potion Buffs (30+)
 - Endless Station Buffs (1+)
 - Endless Healing & Mana Potions (30+)
 - Endless Consumables (999+)
 - Endless Placeables (999+)
 - Endless Bait (999+)
 - Endless Ammo (999+)
 - Increased max buff slots
 - Faster respawns
 - Full HP on spawn
 - No enemy spawns during bosses
 - Lifeform Analyzer points towards rare creatures
 - Crazy fast mining & placing speeds
 - Items that disable enemy & event spawns
 - Item that increases spawn rates by 10x
 - Item that forces biomes
 - Item that teleports you to your last death
 - Item magnet that sucks all items towards you
 - Items that instantly create structures
 - Item that displays minion and sentry slots
 - Item that changes time
 - Item that advances the moon phase
 - Item that reveals the whole map
 - Item that spawns travelling merchants
 - Weapon autoswing
 - Faster extractinator
 - Increased max stack size
 - Automatic coin quick-stacking
 - Increased coin drops
 - All accessories can be placed in vanity
 - Informational accessories work in banks
 - Boss bags don't drop vanities
 - Flying money trough follows the player
 - Extra fishing lures
 - Non-consumable Boss & Event summons
 - 1 Town NPC who sells almost everything needed
 - 1 Town NPC who sells a ton of modded items
 - Friendly NPCs don't take damage
 - NPC happiness removal
 - Editable celestial tower shield health

Warning on Mod Support:
If you have more than one of the supported mods enabled there is a good chance that the NPC shops will overflow.

Warning on Boss-dependent sales:
You have to beat modded bosses with this mod enabled in order for the NPC to sell those items.
